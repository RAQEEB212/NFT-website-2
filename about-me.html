The Story Of Aksyed art Gallery

Experienced, creative and motivated social activist, artist and painter who has crafted random artwork and worked on all mediums of arts including water colors, oil colors, oil pastels soft pastels, charcoal and painted almost 2000 paintings based on creative artworks and also has made 100+ sculptures in clay, plaster of Paris, wood and straws.

Over 20 year's experience, as a Creative Artist, organized 15+ group art exhibitions and 20+ solo art exhibitions and sold 500+ own paintings and portraits at urban and rural vicinities of Pakistan.
